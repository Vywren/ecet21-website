About This Project

This webpage is part of a trainer for electric motors and drives, sponsored jointly by HESCO and the UCONN School of Engineering. the goal is to teach any literate human about the joys of using motors in an engaging, safe and digestible way. For this purpose, we have prepared a number of training modules and labs to both inform the user and provide some real world practice. If you are having trouble navigating this site, please refer to the "Help" section in the top right corner.
