Introduction

What they can expect to learn by completing the experiment (objectives). Provides them with any extra information they need to know before going into the experiment.

Procedure

List of steps they should follow - what we want them to do/find at each step. Would ideally include images of some steps to help them understand better what they are supposed to be doing.

Intended Results

This section will contain what information, data, and so-on they should have gathered at each step in the experiment. That way they can check their answers to ensure they completed the experiment correctly.

Summary

A brief summary of what they did and what they should have learned. Also will include anything else we should mention about the motor in relation to the physical system.
